Upstream Technical Solutions

  • Home
  • About

Established in 2016, Upstream Technical Services initially focused on meeting the growing contracting needs of the oil and gas, chemical, and petrochemical industries in the Middle East. Our founders, with over 30 years of combined experience, set the foundation for a company committed to innovation and excellence. By 2018, we expanded our service offerings to include comprehensive solutions in construction, maintenance, and inspection, leveraging strategic partnerships to execute projects across the Middle East and India. This period marked our growth as a reliable provider in the industry. In 2020, we achieved ISO 18001 and ISO 14001 certifications, reflecting our commitment to the highest standards in Health, Safety, and Environmental management. These certifications underscore our dedication to maintaining exceptional operational standards. Today, Upstream Technical Services operates with a focus on advanced solutions tailored to the oil and gas, power generation, and industrial sectors. We continue to drive innovation and deliver excellence, aiming to be pioneers in EPC contracting through quality, safety, and customer satisfaction. Through strategic partnerships and a dedicated team, we strive to exceed client expectations and foster long-term success.
